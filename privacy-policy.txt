PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Liquidfy ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our service, available at https://liquidfy.app (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you register for and use our Service, we may collect the following information:

- Account Information: Email address, name, and password
- Shopify Store Information: Store domain, store name, and store access tokens (required for integration)
- Payment Information: Billing details, subscription plan, and payment history (processed securely through Stripe)
- User-Generated Content: Custom modules, sections, code snippets, and configurations you create within the Service
- Communication Data: Messages you send to us through contact channels, feedback forms, or email

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you access or use our Service, we automatically collect:

- Usage Information: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the Service, and interaction data
- Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, device identifiers, and referring URLs
- Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage patterns
- Log Data: Server logs including access times, error reports, and performance metrics

1.3 Information from Third Parties

- Shopify Integration: When you connect your Shopify store, we receive store data, product information, theme details, and metafield data as necessary to provide our Service
- Payment Processor: Stripe provides us with transaction confirmations and subscription status updates
- Analytics Providers: We use third-party analytics services to understand how users interact with our Service

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 To Provide and Maintain the Service
- Create and manage your account
- Process your subscription and payments
- Sync modules and sections with your Shopify store
- Store and manage your custom code and configurations
- Provide customer contact and respond to your inquiries

2.2 To Improve and Optimize the Service
- Analyze usage patterns and user behavior
- Develop new features and functionality
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Conduct research and testing to improve user experience

2.3 To Communicate with You
- Send transactional emails (account confirmations, password resets, billing notifications)
- Provide updates about new features, modules, and service improvements
- Send promotional communications (you can opt-out at any time)
- Respond to your questions and contact requests

2.4 To Ensure Security and Prevent Fraud
- Monitor for suspicious activity and unauthorized access
- Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
- Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent violations
- Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

2.5 For Legal and Compliance Purposes
- Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
- Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- Respond to law enforcement requests and court orders

3. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers
We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our Service:
- Shopify: For store integration and theme extension deployment
- Stripe: For payment processing and subscription management
- Vercel: For hosting and infrastructure services
- MongoDB Atlas: For secure database storage
- Supabase: For authentication and database services (Shopify app)
- Analytics Providers: For usage analytics and performance monitoring

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

3.2 Business Transfers
If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, asset sale, or bankruptcy, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:
- Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders, warrants)
- Requests from government authorities or law enforcement
- Situations involving potential threats to safety or security
- Protection of our legal rights and enforcement of our policies

3.4 With Your Consent
We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Data Storage
- Your account data is stored on secure MongoDB Atlas servers
- Shopify app data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols
- Backups are performed regularly to prevent data loss

4.2 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
- Encryption of sensitive data (passwords, access tokens)
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Access controls and authentication mechanisms
- Monitoring for unauthorized access and suspicious activity

4.3 Data Retention
We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:
- Account data: Retained until you delete your account
- Usage logs: Retained for up to 12 months for analytics and troubleshooting
- Payment records: Retained as required by law and accounting standards (typically 7 years)
- Deleted account data: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion, except where retention is required by law

5. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

5.1 Access and Update Your Information
You can access and update your account information at any time through your account settings.

5.2 Delete Your Account
You can request deletion of your account by contacting us at contact@liquidfy.app. Upon deletion:
- Your account and associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days
- Active subscriptions will be canceled
- Modules and sections synced to your Shopify store will remain but will no longer be updated

5.3 Opt-Out of Marketing Communications
You can unsubscribe from promotional emails by:
- Clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email

- Contacting us directly at contact@liquidfy.app

Note: You cannot opt-out of transactional emails related to your account and service usage.

5.4 Cookie Preferences
You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies
- Disabling cookies may affect some features of the Service
- You can opt-out of third-party analytics cookies through your browser or privacy tools

5.5 Data Portability
You can request a copy of your data in a portable format by contacting us at contact@liquidfy.app. We will provide your data within 30 days of your request.

11. SPECIFIC PROVISIONS FOR SHOPIFY APP USERS

11.1 Data Access and Usage
When you install our Shopify app, we access and use the following data:
- Store metafields (read and write access for module/section storage)
- Theme files (read access to understand integration points)
- Store information (basic details for account linking)
- Product data (optional, only when product data injection is enabled)

11.2 Data Synchronization
We automatically sync your modules and sections with your Shopify store:
- Synchronization occurs in real-time when you add, update, or remove content
- Synced data is stored in your Shopify store's metafields
- You maintain full control over this data through your Shopify admin

11.3 Theme Extensions
Our theme app extensions (blocks) render your modules and sections on your storefront:
- Blocks access metafield data to display your custom code
- Product data injection (when enabled) is processed client-side in the browser
- No customer data from your store visitors is collected or transmitted to our servers

11.4 Uninstalling the App
If you uninstall our Shopify app:
- Access to your store data will be immediately revoked
- Your Liquidfy account remains active (unless you separately delete it)
- Metafields created by our app will remain in your Shopify store
- You can manually remove these metafields through Shopify admin if desired
